Privacy Policy for Smart Mom

Introduction

Welcome to Smart Mom. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal and medical information. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data while using our application.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, contact details, and profile information provided during registration.
  • Medical Data: Pregnancy history, medical records, BP readings, hemoglobin levels, and diagnostic test results.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you interact with the app, including session duration and features used.

How We Use Your Information

  • Monitoring antenatal conditions like anemia and hypertension.
  • Providing personalized health recommendations and tracking patient progress.
  • Facilitating communication between doctors and antenatal mothers.
  • Enhancing app functionality and user experience.

Data Security

  • All medical data is securely stored with encryption protocols.
  • Access to personal and medical data is restricted to authorized users only.
  • Regular security audits and updates are conducted to protect your information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • When required by law or regulatory authorities.
  • With your explicit consent for collaboration with healthcare providers or research institutions.

Your Rights

  • The right to access and review your stored medical data.
  • The right to request data deletion through app settings.
  • The right to withdraw consent for data collection.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be communicated through app updates.
